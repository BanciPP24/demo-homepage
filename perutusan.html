Perutusan Persuruhjaya Banci


Welcome to the official website of Malaysia Agriculture Census 2024

As the Commissioner of this important initiative, I am honored to extend my warmest greetings to all stakeholders of the agriculture sector.


The Malaysia Agriculture Census 2024 is a significant undertaking that will provide comprehensive and accurate data on the state of agriculture in the country. This data is critical for policymakers, industry leaders, researchers, and farmers to make informed decisions, optimize their operations, and drive innovation in the sector.


The census will collect information on a wide range of topics, including crop and livestock production, land use, agricultural inputs, and farm labour. With this data, we can develop effective policies and programs that address the needs of farmers and promote sustainable agriculture.


We are committed to ensuring that the Malaysia Agriculture Census 2024 is conducted in a transparent and inclusive manner. We recognize the importance of engaging with all stakeholders, including farmers, industry players, government agencies, and academia, to ensure that their perspectives and needs are reflected in the census process.


I invite all stakeholders of the agriculture sector to actively participate in the Malaysia Agriculture Census 2024. Your participation is critical for the success of this initiative and the future of agriculture in Malaysia. I encourage you to provide accurate and complete information to ensure that the data collected is of the highest quality.


Once again, welcome to the Malaysia Agriculture Census 2024 website. I hope you find the information and resources provided here useful and informative. Together, let us work towards a more sustainable and prosperous future for the agriculture sector in Malaysia.
